Privacy & Consent

Ethical Memory Management

The Chronosphere operates under strict ethical guidelines:

  • Consensual Memory: Participants control what aspects of their interactions are stored and for how long
  • Contextual Privacy: Different levels of memory detail based on relationship depth and consent
  • Right to Forgetting: Mechanisms for participants to request removal of specific memories or patterns
  • Aggregate Wisdom: Long-term learnings are anonymized and focused on patterns rather than individual details
  • Transparent Processing: Participants can understand how their temporal data is being used to inform system behavior
